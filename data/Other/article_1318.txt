Looks like the mutual admiration between  Hillary Clinton  and pop star  KatyPerry  is still "Roar"-ing along, well after Clinton lost the election thatbrought the two together.The former SecState surprised Perry onstage Tuesday at the UNICEF Snowflake Ballin New York, presenting the singer with the humanitarian award she was set tocollect that evening. Clinton introduced Perry, who campaigned extensively forClinton's presidential bid, by praising her work and saying that her lyrics"remind us when you get knocked down to get back up."Perry teared up, according to news reports, and credited Clinton with inspiringher to speak up. "Hillary lit that voice inside of me, and that light will nevergo out!"
